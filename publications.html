Key Research Areas

Our publications cover the following main areas:

  • Natural Language Processing for Greek and dialectal varieties
  • Neuro-symbolic AI and hybrid reasoning systems
  • Computational semantics and formal linguistics
  • Digital humanities and classical text analysis
  • Low-resource language technology
  • Knowledge graph construction and reasoning

Datasets and Resources

We develop and maintain several publicly available datasets and resources for the research community

OYXOY Benchmark

Greek Natural Language Inference benchmark for evaluating language understanding models

Greek Regional Dialects Corpus

Comprehensive corpus covering Cypriot, Pontic, Cretan, and Northern Greek dialectal varieties

MEDEA Platform

Tools and resources for computational analysis of ancient Greek texts
