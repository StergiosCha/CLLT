Research Inquiries

For Prospective Students

We welcome applications from motivated students interested in pursuing graduate studies (Master's or PhD) at CLLT. Our research areas include:

  • Natural Language Processing and Inference
  • Type-theoretical semantics and formal linguistics
  • Greek language technology and dialectal NLP
  • Neural-symbolic AI integration
  • Digital humanities and computational philology
  • Low-resource language processing

To apply: Please send your CV, research interests, relevant background, and a brief statement of purpose to Professor Chatzikyriakidis at schatz@uoc.gr.

For Research Collaborations

We are open to research collaborations with:

  • Academic institutions and research groups
  • Industry partners developing NLP applications
  • Research organizations working on under-resourced languages
  • Digital humanities projects requiring computational linguistics expertise

Please contact us to discuss potential collaborative projects, joint funding applications, or visiting researcher opportunities.

For Dataset Access

Most of our datasets (OYXOY, GRDD, Greek FraCaS, etc.) are publicly available on GitHub and Zenodo with permissive licenses. Visit our Datasets page for download links and documentation.

For questions about specific datasets or to request access to unreleased resources, please contact the lab.

For Seminars and Talks

CLLT hosts a regular seminar series featuring invited speakers from around the world, as well as presentations from lab members. We welcome speaker proposals and collaboration on organizing workshops or seminars.

For information about upcoming seminars or to propose a talk, please get in touch.
