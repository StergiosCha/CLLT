Research Inquiries

We welcome inquiries from prospective students, collaborators, and research partners interested in computational linguistics and natural language processing.

For prospective students: If you are interested in pursuing graduate studies (Master's or PhD) at CLLT, please send your CV, research interests, and relevant background to the lab director.

For collaborations: We are open to research collaborations with academic institutions, industry partners, and research organizations. Please contact us to discuss potential projects.

For dataset access: Many of our datasets and resources are publicly available. Contact us for information about specific resources or collaboration opportunities.
