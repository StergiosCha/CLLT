Visit Us

The lab is located at the Department of Philology, University of Crete, in Rethymno. Visitors are welcome by appointment. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit.

Rethymno is a beautiful historic city on the island of Crete, easily accessible by air through Heraklion or Chania airports, with excellent connections to major European cities.
