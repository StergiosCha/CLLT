Join Our Team

We welcome applications from motivated students and researchers interested in computational linguistics, NLP, and language technology at the intersection of theory and practice.

What We Offer

  • Training in both formal linguistics AND computational methods
  • Access to European research networks (CLASP, TALOS, ERC projects)
  • Opportunities to publish in top venues (ACL, EACL, Cambridge, Oxford)
  • Collaboration with leading international researchers
  • Work on under-resourced languages and explainable AI
  • Beautiful location in Crete, Greece

Who We're Looking For

  • PhD Students: Background in linguistics, CS, or related fields
  • Postdocs: Strong publication record in CL/NLP
  • Visiting Researchers: Interest in collaboration
  • MA Students: Passion for language and computation
