GRDD+: Extended Greek Dialectal Dataset Released

Extended version of GRDD with 10 Greek dialectal varieties including Cretan, Cypriot, Pontic, Northern Greek, Greco-Corsican, Griko, Maniot, Heptanesian, Tsakonian, and Katharevusa. Total size: 6.4M words with fine-tuning evaluation across multiple LLM architectures.
